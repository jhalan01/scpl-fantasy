Setup Guide

Connect your Google Sheet to keep scores live for all 10 friends

Step 1 — Your Google Sheet columns

The sheet must have exactly these 9 columns (same as your auction sheet + Points & Adjustment):

Franchise | Player | Team | Role | Multiplier | Paid Amount | Points | Adjustment | Effective Points

The dashboard reads the Effective Points column (col 9) for all scoring — this already includes the 2× multiplier and any adjustments.

Step 2 — Updating after each match

  1. Go to iplt20.com/stats/2026 after a match ends
  2. Find each player's fantasy points and update the Points column in your sheet
  3. The Effective Points column should auto-calculate: = Points * Multiplier + Adjustment
  4. Save the sheet — the dashboard auto-picks it up on next refresh

Step 3 — Publish as CSV

  1. In Google Sheets: File → Share → Publish to web
  2. Change the dropdown from "Web page" to Comma-separated values (.csv)
  3. Click Publish → copy the URL
  4. Paste it in the toolbar above → click Load Sheet

Step 4 — Share the dashboard

Host this HTML file on GitHub Pages or Netlify (both free). Share the link — friends click it, hit ↻ Refresh after each match to see updated standings.

Scoring Rules (built in)

  • Top 10 players by Effective Points count per franchise; rest are benched
  • Power player (Multiplier = 2) — their Effective Points already reflect the 2× in your sheet
  • Franchises with fewer than 10 players — all players count
